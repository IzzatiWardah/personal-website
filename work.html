My Education

I started entering kindergarten when I was just 4 years old. My first kindergarten is FURQAN kindergarten located in Cheras, Kuala Lumpur. There, I learned to read, count, write and sing which is my favorite activity. I also have chinese and indian friends which I comfortable making friends and sharing stories.

After that, when I was 5 and 6 years old, I started entering Othman Islamic Kindergarten because my parents want me to study Islam better. At that time, I diligently understood the Quran, memorized surahs, learned Arabic and other subjects such as faith, morals and so on. I am very happy and grateful to be able to read the Quran at such a young age!

Primary School

SK Taman Midah 1 is one and only my primary school. I have a lot of sweet and bitter memories of 6 years at studying there. I also have a lot of good friends there and classmates who are so sporting and friendly

While studying there, I often got good exam results and managed to sit first class when I was in 5th and 6th grade. Secondary School

During form 1 to 3, I went to school at SMK Bandar Tun Razak, Cheras that is close to my house because I did not want to be far from my parents. Sometimes, I just walk with my friends to go to and back to school. Honestly, I am very grateful because I have many sweet memories when I was in the school.
